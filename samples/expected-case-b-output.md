## Expected Revised HPI Pattern

The Revised HPI should follow this structure:

patient context → symptom timeline → exam findings → objective labs → documented diagnosis → ED treatment → admission reasoning

A good generated HPI should clearly support inpatient admission because the note documents DKA with severe metabolic derangement, altered mental status, continuous insulin therapy, IV fluids, serial monitoring, and ICU-level care.
